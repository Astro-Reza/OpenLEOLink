Explore Features

Coverage Analysis
This feature analyzes satellite beam footprints in low Earth orbit to determine real-time and projected ground coverage. By modeling beam shape, size, overlap, and movement along orbital paths, the platform identifies which geographic areas are served at any given time. Users can assess coverage density, service continuity, and potential gaps, enabling optimization of constellation design and beam allocation for maximum market reach.
Satellite Settings
The Satellite Settings module allows users to configure and simulate key satellite parameters, including orbital altitude, inclination, frequency band, beamwidth, transmit power, and payload characteristics. By adjusting these variables, users can evaluate how technical design choices affect coverage performance, service quality, and operational constraints, supporting informed engineering and commercial trade-off decisions.
Economics Calculation
By combining satellite coverage models with demographic datasets, the platform calculates potential addressable markets, revenue projections, and cost-efficiency metrics such as revenue per beam or per satellite. This enables users to assess the economic viability of different deployment scenarios and prioritize high-impact regions.
Visualization
The Visualization component presents complex orbital, coverage, and economic data through interactive maps, charts, and timelines. Users can explore satellite ground tracks, beam footprints, population density overlays, and revenue estimates in an intuitive visual format. This makes it easier to communicate insights, compare scenarios, and support strategic decision-making for investors, operators, and policymakers.
